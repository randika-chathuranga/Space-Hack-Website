SpaceHack

SpaceHack is one of the most enthusiastic idea hackathons organized by the SEDS Sabra in collaboration with the IEEE chapter of the Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka. SpaceHack will promote space exploration and space-related concepts of the university students and their involvement with community services and serve humanity.

Event Guidelines

The Contestants are required to comply with conditions of the contest rules and regulations,according to the below provisions.



  • Undergraduates of any University or Higher Educational Institute are eligible to participate in this competition.
  • A team must comprise 3 to 5 members
  • Participants in a team should represent the same University or the Higher Educational Institute
  • The proposed idea cannot be changed. The final product may have added features.
  • By registering for this contest, you hereby agree to receive results on the complication depending on the effort.
